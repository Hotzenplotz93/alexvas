Actor, comedian, producer

Nielsen in 1982

Here's a time line of Leslie Nielsen


  • Nielsen was born on 11 February 1926 in Regina, Saskatchewan.
  • Nielsen's career began in dramatic roles on television during "Television's Golden Age"
  • Forbidden Planet became an instant success
  • After Airplane! and The Naked Gun, Nielsen portrayed similarly styled roles in a number of other films.
  • Nielsen produced instructional golf videos
  • Nielsen appeared in more than 100 films and 1,500 television episodes, portraying more than 220 characters.
